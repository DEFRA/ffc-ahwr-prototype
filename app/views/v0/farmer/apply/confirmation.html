What you should do next

Contact the vet to book the review.

You'll need to give the vet your booking reference number.

The vet will contact you to organise testing of your livestock. They will test for:

  • bovine viral diarrhoea (BVD) in dairy and beef cattle
  • porcine reproductive and respiratory syndrome virus (PRRS) in pigs
  • how effective worming treatments are in sheep

If you’re able to do this testing, discuss this with the vet.

The vet will discuss the test results and treatment options with you when they visit.

You'll need to apply for a new booking reference if you do not arrange a review in the next 12 months.

Confirm the record and get paid

After the vet has recorded information about the review, you’ll receive an email. We’ll ask you to sign in to confirm the record is correct and submit your claim.

You’ll receive your payment within 28 days into your Rural Payments account.

Get help with your application

If you have a question about your application contact the Rural Payments Agency. Have your booking reference with you when you call or include it in your email.

  • Telephone: 03000 200 301
  • Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 5pm (except public holidays)
  • ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk
