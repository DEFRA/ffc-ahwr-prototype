Before you start

You'll need the:

  • livestock review booking reference number
  • name of the vet who did the review
  • name of the vet practice
  • vet's Royal College of Veterinary Surgeons (RCVS) registration number
  • date of livestock review
  • disease test results
  • animal numbers
