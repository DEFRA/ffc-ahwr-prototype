Check if you’re eligible

You’re eligible for a livestock review if you’re a cattle, pig and sheep keeper who has more than one of the following:

  • 10 cattle
  • 20 sheep
  • 50 pigs

The livestock must be registered in England. You must have also received Basic Payment Scheme direct payments in the last 12 months.

You are eligible for one review for each species in a 12-month period.

If you need more than one livestock review

You can have more than one review in a 12-month period if:

  • you have more than one animal type - for example, if you have cattle and sheep
  • your livestock is spread across different locations or managed by different farm managers
