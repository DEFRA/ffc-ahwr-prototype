Sprint 8 User journeys What we want to learn

November
  • Eligibility checker
  • Design hypothesis - If we support farmers in understanding their eligibility, we can allow them to contact their vet to arrange a health and welfare review without the need to apply.
  • If vets understand the scheme they can check or may already know if the farm meets the eligible numbers. How would we check BPS and are payment details set up for release 1 though?

November
  • Email/letter
  • Design hypothesis - If we have eligibilty data for checks (CPH and BPS), we have enough to inform farmers of their eligibility for the scheme, saving them the effort of awareness, understanding eligibilty and apply. We know letters can be sent from SAM (Livestock keepers CPH system) and that farmers that claim BPS are used to recieving emails. If the anaysis is done to link CPH to BPS claims we could contact that group and test guidance and next steps and understand if this approach works, also mitegating any risk of ineligible claims.

October
  • Apply for multiple holdings as one business
  • Exploring if there is a user need to apply on behalf of more than one holding. Data suggests 80% of livestock keepers have one holding. We understand there may be a need from the steering group and the pig sector are on the whole bigger businesses with multiple holdings suggests one application for all farms would be a better approach. Challenges are - can we link back and understand all holdings from current business level data?
