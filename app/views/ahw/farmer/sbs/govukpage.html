Funding for the Annual Health and Welfare Review

How to apply for funding for a vet to visit your farm and carry out a health and welfare review of your livestock.

Overview

You can apply for funding for a yearly vet visit that will cover:

  • a check of your biosecurity measures and advice on improving it
  • recommendations about medicines used on the farm
  • a health and welfare review
  • testing for BVD in cattle, resistance to parasite treatments in sheep and PRRSv in pigs
  • advice on access to grants and payments

You and your vet will agree on the best actions for your farm.

The vet will also collect data that will be used to improve understanding of the health and welfare of the national stock. The data will not be used for inspection purposes.
