Get ready for a livestock review

The vet will contact you to organise disease testing for your livestock before the review. The vet will test for:

  • bovine viral diarrhoea (BVD) in dairy and beef cattle
  • anthelmintic resistance to parasite treatments in sheep
  • porcine reproductive and respiratory syndrome virus (PRRSv) in pigs

The vet will discuss the test results and treatment options with you when they visit.

The vet may also ask you to answer some pre-visit questions. They’ll use this information to make the review as useful as possible.
